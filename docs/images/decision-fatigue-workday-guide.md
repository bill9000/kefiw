# decision-fatigue-workday-guide

- **Slug**: decision-fatigue-workday-guide
- **Headline**: The tiny choices that quietly drain your best decisions
- **Emotion**: Unease
- **Promise**: Make small choices feel visible before they reach the important decision.

## Prompt

Create a 16:9 editorial image for a decision-fatigue guide. Show an adult at a desk facing many small abstract choice cards, while one larger important decision card waits in the background. The mood is uneasy but practical: the tiny choices are draining capacity before the big choice arrives. Soft realistic lighting, no readable text, no logos, no app UI.

## Negative prompt

No readable cards, no gambling visuals, no medical diagnosis symbols, no brand marks, no phone UI, no watermark.
