# task-switching-focus-guide

- **Slug**: task-switching-focus-guide
- **Headline**: The focus-block mistake that makes a full calendar useless
- **Emotion**: Frustration
- **Promise**: Show why fewer contexts beat a longer but fragmented work block.

## Prompt

Create a 16:9 social discovery image for a task-switching and focus guide. Show an adult worker surrounded by several open notebooks and devices, then one clean notebook under a focused beam of light. The mood moves from frustration to control: too many contexts shrink into one protected block. Editorial realism, warm shadows, no readable text, no logos, no screenshots.

## Negative prompt

No readable calendar entries, no app UI, no brand logos, no medical imagery, no exaggerated burnout scene, no watermark.
